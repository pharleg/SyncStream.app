About

Built by people who hate broken feeds.

SyncStream started as a fix for a real problem — a Wix merchant with products on Google that hadn't updated in weeks. It became an app.

Why SyncStream exists

Wix is a legitimate e-commerce platform. Hundreds of thousands of merchants run real stores on it. But the native tools for getting products onto Google Shopping and Meta have always been incomplete — and when they fail, they fail quietly. You don't find out a product is rejected until you check your Merchant Center and see a wall of red.

SyncStream was built to close that gap. Dual-platform support, real-time sync via webhooks, and compliance validation that runs before anything reaches Google or Meta. Errors surface in your dashboard, not in a rejection notice three days later.

The AI layer

Thin product descriptions are one of the most common reasons Google Shopping performance underdelivers. The title and description are what Google uses to match your products to search queries. If your Wix description is two sentences, you're leaving a lot on the table.

SyncStream uses Anthropic's Claude API to expand product descriptions before sync. You control whether AI enhancement is applied, and you review before anything goes live. The goal is better data in, better results out — not AI for its own sake.
